About

A bit about me

UX/Product Designer based in Kathmandu

I'm Sichu Maharjan, a UX/Product Designer at UXCam where I work on shaping analytics tools that help product teams understand user behavior. My focus is on turning complex data workflows into intuitive, clear interfaces.

I believe great design sits at the intersection of user needs, business goals, and technical feasibility. I enjoy working closely with engineers and PMs to ship solutions that are both thoughtful and practical.

Outside of work, I'm drawn to typography, visual systems, and the occasional side project that lets me write code.
